The Scrum metholodogy used by the development team

What is scrum (Scrum.org) Scrum is a lightweight process framework for agile development that allows for incremental development. We had daily stand up meetings, where we discussed progress since the last meeting, what team members plan to do before the next meeting (selected from the sprint backlog), and any pertinent issues. Our team also had regular sprint reviews and retrospectives (at the end of every sprint).
